What happens during a lesson?

Every lesson is 1-on-1 over video call (Zoom or Google Meet) and tailored to you. A typical lesson includes:

  • A quick warm-up conversation in Urdu to ease in
  • Review of previous vocabulary or grammar
  • New material introduced with examples and practice
  • Speaking practice based on real-life scenarios
  • A short recap and homework suggestion for next time

What you can expect

After your first month — basic greetings, everyday phrases, and simple conversation.

After 3 months — comfortable holding short conversations and understanding spoken Urdu in context.

After 6+ months — confident conversation, solid reading/writing foundation, and natural expression.
